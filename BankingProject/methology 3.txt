methods of research

online research - internet is a good way to start study. some information is available to help the proponents construct a strong foundation for the proposed system. the proponents gain sufficient knowledge and materials for the project.

review existing literature - the proponents will conduct a literature review of existing studies related to school tap id system. 

record data - record the data from the interviews and recommendation. this may involve taking notes, recording the conversation.

Collect Data - The proponents will collect data. This may involve conducting interviews with the proponents and asking question s in order to get information from students, teachers, and security guard. 

analyze data - once the proponents collected the data, they will analyze the data and compare the attendance and security measures before and after the implementation of the school tap Id System.
